## Job Description
Director, Infrastructure at Reveleer. Remote or Glendale CA. $175K-$225K. Multi-cloud healthcare SaaS platform operating primarily in AWS, with workloads in Azure and GCP. Lead cloud operations, Kubernetes/EKS, networking, deployment automation, observability, and platform reliability. Own cloud infrastructure operations across AWS, Azure, and GCP. Lead EKS and container platform operations. Manage Lambda and serverless workloads. Oversee CI/CD, blue/green, and canary deployments. Lead observability and monitoring using New Relic, CloudWatch. Own network infrastructure: VPC design, routing, firewall/security group rules, load balancers, ingress/egress policies. Drive cost optimization for compute, storage, and data transfer. Enhance infrastructure security (IAM, RBAC, secrets, network security, WAF). Oversee AI/ML infrastructure (AWS Bedrock, Google Gemini). Lead incident management, on-call rotations, post-incident improvement. Collaborate with Security and Engineering for HIPAA/HITRUST compliance. Lead and mentor distributed infrastructure team across US and India. Requirements: 10+ years in cloud infrastructure, DevOps, or SRE; 3+ years leadership for SaaS or multi-cloud. Strong AWS, Kubernetes/EKS, cloud networking, IAM, distributed systems. Experience with monitoring tools and incident response. IaC (Terraform, CloudFormation).
