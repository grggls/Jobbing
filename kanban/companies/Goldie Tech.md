- 'EVERYTHING STILL NEEDS TO BE BUILT' — No codebase to inherit from Marcel. This is greenfield. The CTO role is building from zero, not taking over an existing system.
- BUSINESS MODEL: B2B2C — Goldie provides white-label gold investment infrastructure to banks via API. Revenue from transaction spread + yearly storage/management fees for physical gold custody.
- BUFFER CONCEPT: Goldie retains 3.5% of liquidated gold (not cash) as a reserve. Buffer compounds as liquidation volume increases — protects against run scenarios. Gold-denominated, so EUR value fluctuates with gold price.
- TAX OPTIMIZATION AS PRODUCT DIFFERENTIATOR: German Spekulationsfrist — gold held >12 months reclassified from speculative to savings, zero capital gains tax. FIFO lot selection optimizes which holdings are sold first. This is customer-visible value.
- COMPETITOR: Solit Kapital — largest competitor, has Santander integration. Also Argo Vault, Rush Gold, Goldex, M2P Fintech in adjacent spaces.
