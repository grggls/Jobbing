## Job Description
Software Engineer, Platform Operations
Design, build, and operate core infrastructure enabling Planet's engineering teams to reliably deploy and scale services. Design and implement Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) solutions for secure and scalable operation. Work on major platform modernization initiatives (migration from legacy tooling). Manage cloud-based infrastructure services, notably Kubernetes clusters. Enhance and maintain observability using Grafana and other tools to establish Service Level Objectives (SLOs). Implement improvements for core systems: GKE clusters, public API gateway, managed infrastructure. Collaborate with software engineering teams to refine developer experience (DevEx).
Required Technical Skills:
- Deep understanding of Kubernetes, underlying compute systems, and Linux
- Working knowledge of public clouds, particularly GCP or AWS
- Experience with CI/CD tools (GitLab, ArgoCD)
- Experience with Configuration Management (Terraform, Crossplane) and GitOps principles
- Experience building services in Go and Python
- Familiarity with Git, Docker, CI/CD workflows
Preferred Qualifications:
- Experience in operational management and development of core platform systems
- Experience maintaining highly available or operationally resilient infrastructure at large scales
- Experience across multiple clouds
Full-time, remote (US-based). If near office, expected to work in office 3 days/week. Planet Labs operates the largest fleet of Earth-imaging satellites (~200+ satellites).
