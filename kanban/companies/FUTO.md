## Job Description
Senior Infrastructure Engineer (Storage & Kubernetes) — Immich
Build and operate backend infrastructure for Immich, a high-performance self-hosted photo and video management platform. Immich is an open-source alternative to Google Photos/iCloud Photos with a strong privacy and self-hosting focus.
Technical Focus:
- Storage infrastructure and systems architecture
- Kubernetes deployment and management
- End-to-end encrypted (E2EE) infrastructure components
- Bare-metal and self-hosted infrastructure (privacy mandate)
- Go, Rust, TypeScript
Immich joined FUTO in May 2024, allowing the core team to work full-time while maintaining complete autonomy. Can be deployed on Kubernetes using official Helm chart.
FUTO is a privacy-focused technology organization founded by Eron Wolf. Based in Austin, TX. Funds and builds open-source software that respects user privacy and data sovereignty. Key projects include Immich, Grayjay, Circles, FUTO Keyboard, and FUTO Voice.
